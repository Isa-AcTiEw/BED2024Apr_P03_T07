About Us

Welcome to SGoofy Community Club, your hub for fostering community spirit and engagement in our vibrant neighborhood. Established in 2024, our mission is to create a welcoming and inclusive space where residents of all ages can come together, participate in enriching activities, and build lasting connections.

At SGoofy Community Club, we offer a wide range of programs and services designed to meet the diverse interests and needs of our community. From fitness classes and cultural events to educational workshops and recreational activities, there's something for everyone. Our facilities include a state-of-the-art gym, a spacious multipurpose hall, and well-maintained outdoor spaces, all designed to support your physical and social well-being.

Our dedicated team of staff and volunteers is committed to making SGoofy Community Club a place where everyone feels valued and supported. We believe in the power of community and strive to provide opportunities for residents to connect, learn, and grow together.
